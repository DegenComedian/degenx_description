DegenX is a multichain cryptocurrency ecosystem of DeFi products that provides value for both projects and users. Its governance token is $DGNX, which is launched on the avalanche blockchain. The DegenX project has no CEO or projectleader, it is community driven using a DAO. Revenue is distributed to stakers of DegenX. The governance token of DegenX is using liquidity backing: a minimum guaranteed price that only grows. The ecosystem consists of $DGNX, Swap, DAO, bridge, LockeR, StakeX, Factor, NFT and clinic. Bridging and Staking are currently under development, the rest will follow afterwards. The tokenomics are as follow: total supply (across all chains) is 21.000.000. There is a buy and sell fee of 10%. The DegenX project website can be found at https://dgnx.finance.
